Sight of Sound

Sight of Sound is a riso-printed animation interpreting Björk’s Heirloom through experimental visual rhythm. Working with sound as structure, I translated beats, vocals, and ambient textures into layered motion, color pulses, and hand-drawn forms. The project merges cultural motifs from my Thai and my partner’s Chinese backgrounds, creating a hybrid visual language that moves between sound, memory, and print-based animation.
